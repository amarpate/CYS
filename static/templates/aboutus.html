About us

Campus Yard Sale is an online communal marketplace to buy and sell things with your classmates. Textbooks, electronics, and furniture are just a few of the things you can buy! No more searching for used textbooks online, dealing with people you don’t know, or paying outrageous shipping fees. With CYS you are dealing with people from YOUR college or university from the safety and convenience of your campus. Going abroad for a semester and want to rent out your apartment? Post about it on your school’s page and find a new tenant. On Campus Yard Sale, the possibilities are endless as to what you can buy, sell, and trade. Our goal is to be on every campus across the country, and help make the lives of college students just a little easier!
